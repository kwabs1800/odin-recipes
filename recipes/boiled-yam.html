BOILED YAM

How To!

INGREDIENTS

  • Water
  • Yam
  • Salt

STEPS

First off you shouldn't even be here looking up how to make boiled yam. Come on!

  1. Peel off the yam nicely with your knife and cut into sizable pieces. Put these pieces into water and add a little salt.
    2. We're almost Finished...
  2. Now put it on fire and wait for about 25-30 minutes and it's cooked and ready to eat!
